"financial"

Learn more about your financial wellbeing


Get a better understanding of your financial wellbeing so you can take steps to improve it, whether it’s taking control of your finances, handling an unexpected expense or reaching your future goals.

What is financial wellbeing?


It’s how you manage your money both now and for the future, so you’re in control of your financial goals and prepared for the unexpected. We think of it in 3 categories:
Every Day – managing your day-to-day finances like rent or mortgage, transport costs and bills;
Rainy Day – being ready to deal with the unexpected, and having an emergency fund just in case;
One Day – reaching your financial goals, making the most of your money and setting up your future.
